Credit Card FAQ

Q1: How do I apply for a credit card?
A1: Visit any bank branch or apply online via our website.

Q2: What is the annual fee?
A2: Basic card: $50/year, Premium card: $100/year.

Q3: What is the interest rate on credit cards?
A3: 18% per annum on outstanding balances.

Q4: What is the minimum payment due?
A4: 5% of outstanding balance or $25, whichever is higher.

Q5: How can I report a lost or stolen card?
A5: Call our 24/7 hotline immediately to block your card.
